Research

Pollution is something that affects everything whether you like it or not. Pollution can cause a lot of damage that affect the environment, people's health, and the wild life. Technological innovations like solar power can solve the social problem of pollution by reducing the air pollution, water pollution, and the need for finite resources, which are resources that do not renew themself like fossil fuels.

Key Points

  • Pollution is an issue that can be solved with solar power.
  • Pollution in the water is bad for the economy.
  • Air pollution can harm people's health.
  • Solar power affects the environment a lot less.
  • Solar power does not produce greenhouse gases so, it reduces air pollution.
  • Solar power does not use water so, it does not pollute the water.
