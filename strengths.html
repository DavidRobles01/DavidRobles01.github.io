Future Thinker:

Most of my time is spent thinking about what the future could be. I tend to space out a lot b ecause i'm thinking about what could happen if certain actions are made. This then leads to me thinking of what I can do and what I can't do yet. The yet is very important because I don't like to think of things as impossible because no matter how good or bad you are at something there is always room for improvement, so if enough effort and time is put into something you will be able to accomplish what you want to accomplish and this goes for anyone not just me.

I use this strength in everything but mainly in school. For example if I see there is something that interests me during class I would space out during class and just think about the topic that has been said. Also because I know i'm not the best in all the classes I like to think about how things would be if I was the best in some of the classes. Also when there is class discussion I tend to think deep about the topic and sometimes not participate because I am busy thinking about everything.

The way I feel depends on what I am thinking about because sometimes I think about really sad things and it just makes me feel depressed and other times I could be thinking about great things and that makes me feel happy. Also sometimes when I think about school or life I feel stressed.


How can I use this strength:
